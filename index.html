03How I Work
01 / DOCUMENT

Clarity first

I start by writing things down. On Old Grandma I authored the GDD so all five of us were building the same game — clear rules and systems mean fewer misunderstandings and a faster-moving team.

02 / BALANCE

Design for fairness

A game should challenge players, not frustrate them. I build and tune systems — like difficulty models — so the experience stays fair and enjoyable from start to finish.

03 / ITERATE

Let players guide me

I trust playtests over assumptions. Watching real players, gathering feedback, and reading what's actually happening tells me where to adjust — then I refine and test again.
